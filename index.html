What It Does

Correctify corrects grammar, spelling, and punctuation while preserving your writing style and formatting. It's a minimal, distraction-free menubar app that works on macOS, Windows, and Linux.

Features

Menubar App

Lives in your system tray/menubar for instant access. Single click to show/hide. No dock icon on macOS. Launch at startup option available. Lightweight and always accessible without cluttering your desktop or dock.

Writing Style Selector

Choose from 5 different writing styles: Grammar Only, Formal, Informal, Collaborative, or Concise. Your preference persists across sessions. Perfect for adapting corrections to your specific needs and context.

Privacy First + Secure Storage

v1.1.0: API keys now stored with OS-level encryption in secure app data directory. No text storage, no database, no analytics or tracking. Your data remains yours. Usage statistics stay local. Complete transparency with zero data collection.

Global Shortcut with Auto Copy/Paste

Correct text from any app using Cmd+Shift+] (macOS) or Ctrl+Shift+] (Windows/Linux). Optional auto copy/paste feature automatically copies and pastes corrected text in place - no manual Cmd+C or Cmd+V needed. Fully customizable.

14 AI Models + Free Options

v1.1.0: Choose from 14 models across 4 providers - OpenAI (GPT-5, GPT-5 Mini, GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-3.5 Turbo), Anthropic (Claude 3.5), Mistral, and OpenRouter. 4 free models available (no credit card). Pay-per-use for premium models. Intelligent fallback to free models on error.

Sound Notifications

Optional audio feedback for all notifications with distinct sounds for different notification types. Enable/disable in Settings. Works across all platforms. Provides clear audio cues for processing, completion, and error states.

Getting Started

1

Get API Key(s) from Your Preferred Provider(s)

Choose one or more providers based on your needs:

OpenRouter - Get FREE API Key (4 free models, no credit card!)

OpenAI | Anthropic | Mistral (Premium models, pay-per-use)

2

Configure Correctify

Open the app from your system tray/menubar, click Settings, and add your API key(s) in the API Keys tab. Your keys are stored securely with OS-level encryption.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: You can configure multiple providers and switch between them anytime!

3

Choose Your Model & Writing Style

Select from 10 available models (paid or free) and 5 writing styles (Grammar, Formal, Informal, Collaborative, Concise). Your preferences are saved automatically.

4

Start Correcting

Type or paste text in the app, or use the global shortcut (Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+]) from any application to correct text instantly with auto copy/paste.

In-App Correction

1

Type or paste text into the input area

2

Click "Correct" or press Cmd+Enter (Mac) / Ctrl+Enter (Windows/Linux)

3

See the corrected text appear below and click Copy to use it

Global Shortcut (Recommended)

With Auto Copy/Paste

1

Select text in any app (just highlight it, no copying needed)

2

Press Cmd+Shift+] (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+] (Windows/Linux)

3

Wait for notification - corrected text automatically pastes in place!

Note: You'll need to grant Accessibility permissions for Auto Copy/Paste. Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility and enable Correctify.

Manual Mode

1

Select and copy text in any app (Cmd+C / Ctrl+C)

2

Press your configured shortcut

3

Wait for notification and paste the corrected text (Cmd+V / Ctrl+V)

Privacy First

What We Do

  • Send your text to OpenAI for correction
  • Store your API key locally on your device
  • Preserve your writing style and formatting

What We Don't Do

  • Log, store, or track your text
  • Collect analytics or telemetry
  • Use databases or external storage

Your API key stays local and is never logged. Your data remains yours.
